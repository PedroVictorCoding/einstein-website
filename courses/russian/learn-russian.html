Greetings


Let's learn how to greet someone in Russian

Learning a new language can be very challenging, and there are many things that need special attention when learning, so it is very important that you learn some very common words and phrases like greetings to understand better how the language works.

{TL;DR}

Hello - Привет - Privet

Good Morning - Доброе утро - Dobroye utro

Good Afternoon - Добрый день - Dobryy den'

Good Evening - Добрый вечер - Dobryy vecher

How are you? - Как твои дела - Kak tvoi dela

What's your name? - Как вас зовут? - Kak vas zovut
