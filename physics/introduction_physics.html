Kinetic and Potential Energy


Kinetic and Potential Energy is the energy associated with movement or the absence of it;

Examples of kinetic energy are a car moving or a rocket launching. All of them have energy. All of them have the ability to change from the effect of forces.

Examples of potential energy are an apple hanging in a tree.

When you drop an object, there is a force acting upon it, whenever you grab your phone, you and the phone are using forces. Now you can call yourself of a Jedi because you can use the force.

Forces are interactions that change the movement of a certain object and are expressed as Force (F) = Mass (m) * Acceleration (a).

A Force that changes an object compared to its beginning state is said to provide work. Work also is defined by the use of a formula Work (W) = Force (F) * Distance (d).
